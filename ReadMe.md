# CSCE 483: Car Rider Student Pick-Up System ( Team #3: The Wranglers )
This repository contains all design materials, documentation, code and media developed for the **Car Rider Student Pick Up System**. 
Team 3: The Wranglers
Haya Abusada, Jake Hewett, Jayden Pearson, Jack Uehlinger, Sara Vorhaben

---
## Objectives 
 - The system will allow parents to update their information (license plate numbers, approved adults for pickup, car rider schedules) via a website with approval from a staff member of the school.
 - The system will read the marker on the car (license plate, QR code, nametag) as it enters the pickup line and send the information to a server.
 - The server will identify the parent and child associated with the car marker, and update a display at the entrance of the pickup line to direct the car to the proper station.
 - The laptop at the front of the school is updated to inform the students and staff which child is being picked up, and which zone to go to.
